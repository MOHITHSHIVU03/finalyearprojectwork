PROJECT DESCRIPTION

FLOW CHART OF OUR PROJECT

A web application that analyzes text documents(patient’s discharge report) to identify personal health identifiers that lead to categorizing the document into sensitive or non_sensitive. The system preserves patient's personal information present in sensitive records with the help of Anonymization which is of two types, namely Generalization and Sanitization. These processes anonymize the sensitive record completely and partially respectively. The non-sensitive documents are suppressed and sent for summarization. Whereas anonymized documents can either be downloaded directly or sent for the summarization process based on the user's choice. The summarization process further improves the readability of the document by getting the most relevant information such as Disease, Medication, Treatment that are related to the patient’s discharge report.
